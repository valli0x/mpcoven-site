How it works

From pairing to atomic settlement

01

Pair & authenticate

Two parties pair addresses. MetaMask EIP-191 login, JWT sessions with replay protection.

02

Distributed keygen

CMP (ECDSA) and FROST (Schnorr) generate split keys — up to 100 shared accounts per pair.

03

Escrow verification

Signatures are cross-validated via the pollination pattern; the agent verifies both parties before release.

04

Atomic settlement

Verified signatures release funds on both chains — no counterparty risk, no intermediary.
